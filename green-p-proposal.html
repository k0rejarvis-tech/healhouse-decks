The Land Exchange Logic

A land swap in spirit: public land value exchanged for affordable housing delivery.

The City can treat the Green P parcel as a public-benefit lever. Instead of asking Heal House to simply replace land value with cash alone, the transaction can recognize the affordable housing and community benefit being delivered in return.

Public Protections

The City can transfer land without losing accountability.

The land transaction can be structured around transparent public-benefit safeguards, without turning the City into a joint-venture partner or project operator.

Possible safeguards

Affordable housing covenant registered against the project.
Non-profit role documented in the delivery structure.
Public-benefit reporting to the City.
Milestones tied to planning and delivery progress.

What this avoids

No City operating burden.
No complicated ownership partnership.
No unnecessary complexity in the first conversation.
No dilution of the core public outcome.
