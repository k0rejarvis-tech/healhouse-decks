ICP 1

Founders & creators

They have ideas, offers, audiences, half-built products, and messy execution. Crew gives instant clarity; Supersize gives implementation.

  • Hooks: “Validate your idea in 10 minutes.”
  • Artifacts: app blueprint, pitch deck, GTM pack, sprint scope.
  • Paid path: Build sprint / MVP sprint / launch pack.
ICP 2

Real estate / investment operators

They need fast diligence, memo creation, risk framing, investor-ready narratives, and repeatable deal workflows.

  • Hooks: “Turn a deal into an investor-grade memo.”
  • Artifacts: diligence memo, risk controls, deck, capital narrative.
  • Paid path: Strategy Pack / investor deck / underwriting workflow.
90-day member journey

The transformation K0RE sells

Days 1–30: Baseline the human/system

  • KJ onboarding, goals, memory, projects, habits, opportunities.
  • Member starts using K0RE as daily command center.
  • Crew runs turn raw thoughts into first private artifacts.

Days 31–60: Create the assets

  • Best ideas become product blueprints, GTM packs, deal memos, workflows, decks.
  • Member sees compounding value from memory + repeated Crew use.
  • Supersize invites operator review on high-potential artifacts.

Days 61–90: Build or own the winners

  • K0RE scopes and prices implementation sprints.
  • Member chooses build, pause, or submit to K0RE Studio.
  • Selected opportunities move into revenue share, licensing, equity, or co-build agreements only with explicit consent.
Ownership model

How K0RE benefits by ownership without breaking trust

User data and ideas stay private by default. K0RE only participates in upside when the member explicitly opts into a Studio/co-build agreement.

Implementation fee

Straight paid build work. Lowest friction, immediate revenue.

Revenue share

K0RE builds or launches an asset and receives a percentage of revenue.

Licensing

K0RE packages repeatable systems, templates, or automations for broader use.

Equity / JV

Only for exceptional projects with clear agreement, scope, and governance.

90-day launch plan

GTM sequence

Days 1–30: Proof + demos

  • Create 10 flagship artifacts across product, real estate, GTM, automation.
  • Record short demo videos: idea → artifact → Supersize.
  • Launch landing page around Crew.
  • Use founder/operator outreach to book 10 pilot calls.

Days 31–60: Paid pilots

  • Sell 5–10 Strategy Packs.
  • Convert 2–3 into Build Sprints.
  • Collect before/after case studies.
  • Tighten package pricing and scope templates.

Days 61–90: Scale wedge

  • Publish case studies and artifact gallery examples.
  • Add referral loop: “Share your artifact.”
  • Launch niche pages: AI business plan, real estate memo, app blueprint, workflow automation.
  • Start outbound to agencies, investors, RE operators, founders.
