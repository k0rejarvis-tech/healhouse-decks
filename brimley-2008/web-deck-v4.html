07 / 12

Answer the hard questions before they become objections.

The lawyer brief already identifies the questions that matter. v4 brings those objections forward rather than burying them.

Year 20 buyout

Formula-based on the original $600K retained equity amount, not speculative market value. Protects certainty; caps upside.

Insolvency / default

Landowner and CMHC step-in rights; operator replacement subject to CMHC approval.

Assignment

Estate succession and corporate restructuring permitted; speculative resale restricted.

Guarantee exposure

Limited to construction phase and released at stabilization: 90% occupancy + 1.15× DSCR for 3 months.

Affordability covenant

Minimum 30% of units below 80% CMHC Median Market Rent; remaining 70% at market rents in underwriting.

Funding failure

LOI/PSA conditional on BCH approval. If declined, no obligation on either side; Ubuntu bears application costs.
