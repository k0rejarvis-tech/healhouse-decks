## 2. Proposed transaction concept

Landowner contributes the property into a single-purpose affordable rental housing structure rather than completing a conventional fee-simple sale. The structure is intended to provide upfront cash, retained equity participation, and a long-term net-cash-flow stream.

## 3. Day 1 consideration

- Cash to landowner at first construction draw: **$1,400,000**
- Retained landowner equity basis: **$600,000**
- Total modeled land contribution/economic basis: **$2,000,000**

## 4. Project scenario

- Primary scenario: **30 units / 6 storeys**
- Alternative scenario: **24 units / reduced footprint**
- Final unit count, massing, parking, GFA, code compliance, and approvals: **subject to municipal, architectural, engineering, BCH and CMHC review**

## 5. Distribution concept

- Landowner share of project net cash flow: **60% for 20 years**
- Project/operator share: **40%**
- DSCR downside waterfall: if DSCR remains below **1.15x** for two consecutive quarters, landowner share increases to **80%** until reserves are replenished, subject to definitive documents.

## 6. Year 20 mechanics

- Formula-based return/buyout tied to the original retained equity amount of **$600,000**.
- Exact transfer, buyout, extension, refinancing, and sale mechanics to be confirmed by counsel.
