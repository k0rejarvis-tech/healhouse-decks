City of Toronto Staff Briefing

Green P into affordable housing.

A short internal deck to help colleagues get on side. Heal House is asking the City to support a pathway for the Green P parcel to become part of a broader affordable housing and community-wellness solution — delivered with a non-profit partner, clear public-benefit protections, and a stronger long-term revenue story.

The Opportunity

A parking parcel can unlock a deeper civic outcome.

The Green P lands sit inside a larger Lakeshore opportunity. On their own, they serve a narrow civic function. As part of the Heal House assembly, they can become affordable housing, wellness infrastructure, parking support, and a permanent neighbourhood benefit.

Today: limited use as a standalone municipal parcel.
Unlocked: part of a complete site plan with housing, services, and parking.
Outcome: underused public land converted into long-term municipal revenue and visible social benefit.

The Proposal

Heal House secures Green P. The City secures affordable housing outcomes.

The Green P transfer, sale, lease, or land-swap-equivalent should be structured around the affordable housing outcome Toronto wants delivered — with Heal House assembling the site and a non-profit partner stewarding affordability.

Public-benefit logic

City: defines land pathway, conditions, and safeguards.
Heal House: integrates Green P into the complete-site plan.
Non-profit: delivers and stewards the affordable commitment.
Outcome: land value converted into housing, tax generation, and community benefit.

The Short Story

Toronto can turn a parking parcel into affordable housing by letting Heal House secure Green P, assemble the complete site, and deliver affordability through a non-profit partner.

Possible safeguards

  • Affordable housing covenant.
  • Non-profit delivery role.
  • Public-benefit reporting.
  • Milestones tied to planning progress.

What this avoids

  • No City operating burden.
  • No complicated ownership partnership.
  • No unnecessary complexity in the first conversation.
  • No dilution of the core public outcome.
