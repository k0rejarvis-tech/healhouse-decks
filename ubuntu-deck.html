Slide 12 — Risk & Mitigation

Risk Assessment

  • Government Program: Build Canada Homes terms may evolve.
    Mitigation: Lock contribution agreement at closing.
  • 50-Year CMHC: Slower principal paydown on CMHC tranche.
    Mitigation: Lower debt service = higher cash flow and DCR coverage.
  • Land Refi Risk: 95% LTV on $20M leaves thin equity cushion.
    Mitigation: Lakeshore corridor has strong upward trajectory; $3M uplift from approvals already baked in; Green P adds $6M with no acquisition cost.
  • Non-Profit Governance: Co-ownership requires clear operating agreements.
    Mitigation: Ubuntu Land Trust provides established legal framework.
  • 20-Year Horizon: Longer commitment.
    Mitigation: Quarterly distributions from Year 1; investor bump provides day-one value.

Why Ubuntu Wins
